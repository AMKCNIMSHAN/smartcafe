About Smart Cafe

Smart Cafe is one of the oldest coffee houses, known for its exceptional quality and welcoming ambiance. We have grown rapidly and now have multiple branches.

Our Coffee Story

Lounge is yet another venture from the Sri Lankan entrepreneur and E-Commerce Guru Dulith Herath. Harithe Lounge came into existence mainly because of his addiction to coffee, any workaholic would agree and bear witness to a condition called “Procaffeinating” the tendency to not start anything without a cup of coffee. Lounge is a startup which was created to cater to the true authentic coffee shop experience, by emphasizing on the high-quality coffee, which we have not compromised whatever the situation we faced. The true reason for the success of Harithe Lounge is its workforce, some employees being a part of the Harithe team since its inception, we at Harithe appreciate all employees starting from the security. With our humble beginnings at Harithe Lounge Jawatte, throughout these five years Harithe Lounge has grown strength to strength expanding our branches to Barns Place, Nawala, Bambalapitiya, Kandy, Mount Lavinia, Nugegoda, Palawaththa, Peradeniya, Ambuluwawa, Fort, Jawaththa, Weli Park Nugegoda, and Mirihana with ideas of expanding even further to ensure that a good quality cup of coffee is just within your reach.

Vison - To establish Haritha Coffee Shop as a top destination for coffee lovers and an industry leader in efficiency by developing an outstanding online presence that boosts customer engagement and expedites administrative procedures.

Mission - Our goal is to provide administrators with strong tools to manage reservations and the product catalogue at the same time, so that everything runs smoothly. We work to give customers and administrators an enjoyable and hassle-free experience through cutting-edge design and technology.
